How It's Made

From Palm to You

🌴
Harvest
Skilled tappers climb Palmyra palms at dawn to collect fresh sap
🪣
Collect
Fresh sap is carefully collected in clay pots to preserve purity
🔥
Boil
Sap is slowly boiled over wood fire, stirred continuously
🟤
Set
Thickened syrup is poured into moulds and allowed to cool naturally
📦
Pack
Quality checked, weighed, and packed fresh for delivery to your home
